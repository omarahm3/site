Our Values

At Levari, we are committed to excellence in all aspects of our service and we seek to provide the best, most economic and innovative legal solutions to our clients. In summary, our values are based on being:

  • 1

    Dedicated to our clients

    We work hard to understand and accommodate your needs and even harder to exceed your expectations.

  • 2

    Service and solutions-driven

    We are committed to providing reasoned, technically correct, practical, and clear recommendations of how to proceed on any issue on which we are asked to advise.

  • 3

    Private and confidential

    We are strongly committed to protecting the privacy of those who entrust us with their personal information.

  • 4

    Cost Transparency

    We use sensible and efficient working methods with competitive and transparent pricing structures giving you cost certainty and therefore complete budgetary control.

A Few Words About Us

Levari in association with Pitmans is a full service international law firm based in Cairo, Egypt. Levari has the expert knowledge to help drive local and international businesses as well as the experience and the know-how to manage significant foreign investments and cross-border transactions.

In addition, Pitmans, our associate firm, is the largest commercial law firm headquartered in the South East of England. We have 32 partners and over 120 lawyers in our team based in Reading and the City of London. Levari is also a member of MI InterAct Limited and thus we are a part of a powerful international network of lawyers with offices in 17 European cities which enables us to offer an integrated service across Europe.

Associated Offices: Amsterdam, Antwerp, Barcelona, Belfast, Copenhagen, Edinburgh, Egypt, Glasgow, Leipzig, Lisbon, Ljubljana, Madrid, Munich, Nicosia, Paris, Porto, Rome, and Zurich.
